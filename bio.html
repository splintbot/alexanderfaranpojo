Rev. Alexander Faranpojo is a fine teacher/preacher of the Love of God to humanity

with practical steps on how it can translate into new levels of victories and fulfilment in life. For over a decade now, He presides as the general overseer VIRTUES CHRISTIAN CENTRE , Lagos Nigeria and the President VIRTUES INTERNATIONAL MINISTRIES.

His teaching emphasis is on the love of God to humanity and the practical application of the Word of God`s grace in the conventional lives of the people.
